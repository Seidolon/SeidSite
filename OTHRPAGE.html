Age of Empires 2 - Map Scripts

I have dabbled slightly in making custom map scripts for AOE2. I have posted the ones I made below.



Ahvenanmaa

Generates a map with many small forested islands. Build a big navy for this one and be prepared to spread your territory.

DOWNLOAD:

Ahvenanmaa.rms

Dead Marshes

A Tolkien-inspired map, this one generates land, water, and shallows. Be prepared to build a naval force as well as ground troops.

DOWNLOAD:

Dead Marshes.rms

Forest of Suffering

This forest map will indeed make you suffer, as there is a good possibility that you won't even have any contact with the other players. In that case, you will need to cut your way through to the outside world.

DOWNLOAD:

Forest of Suffering.rms
